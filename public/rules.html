Contest Rules
New American Funding reserves the right to modify or terminate the contest at any time without further notice.

1  Eligibility

1.1 Open to legal residents of the United States who are 18 years of age or older at the time of entry.
1.2 Employees of New American Funding (NAF), paid influencers, and paid UGC creators are not eligible to win cash prizes.
1.3 Void where prohibited by law.

2  Entry Requirements

2.1 Participants must follow @newamericanfunding on both Instagram and TikTok.
2.2 Participants must post an original short-form video to Instagram or TikTok using the official hashtag: #HellYeahNAF.
2.3 The video must include the phrase “hell yeah” and the name “New American Funding,” either spoken or shown on screen.
2.4 Video must be posted during the official contest period.
2.5 After posting, participants must send a direct message to @newamericanfunding on the platform used to notify the brand of their entry.

3  Content Guidelines

3.1 All videos must comply with Instagram and TikTok community guidelines and terms of service.
3.2 All content must be the original work of the participant.
3.3 The content must NOT include:

Prohibited

Financial advice from unlicensed individuals
Claims or suggestions of guaranteed loan approval
Specific interest rates, APRs, or loan terms

4  Prizing

4.1 Two (2) winners will be selected each week during the contest period to receive $500 each.
$500 PER WINNER

4.2 Winners will be chosen based on originality, inspiration, and organic reach.
4.3 Only unpaid participants are eligible to win cash prizes.
4.4 Winners will be contacted via direct message and must respond within 5 days to claim their prize.
4.5 Prize payments will be delivered via digital gift card or check, at New American Funding's discretion.

5  Usage Rights

5.1 By entering, participants grant New American Funding the right to repost, share, or use submitted content in its marketing and promotional materials, with or without credit.

6  Disqualification

6.1 New American Funding reserves the right to disqualify any entry for inappropriate content or rule violations.
6.2 Failure to meet any of the above requirements may result in disqualification.
6.3 New American Funding reserves the right to terminate the contest without further notice for any reason.
